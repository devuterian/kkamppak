# Daily Inbox Pressure Review

Use with [REPO.md](../../REPO.md) and [INBOX.md](../../INBOX.md).

## Procedure

1. Read active `IBX-*` entries in `INBOX.md`.
2. Cluster related items; split or merge when it reduces noise.
3. For each cluster: recommend `route` | `research` | `plan` | `discard` | `leave`.
4. Promote only **survived** triage into SPEC, STATUS, PLANS, research, or decisions — not raw inbox dumps.
5. Remove inbox lines once reflected or explicitly dropped; keep stable IDs for provenance references.

## Quality bar

Short outcomes, counts for low-signal clusters, no giant “future ideas” digest.
